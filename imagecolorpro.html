How to Use Image Color PRO

1. Upload Your Image

Drag and drop your image file into the upload area or click to browse your files. We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats up to 10MB in size.

2. Apply Presets or Adjust Manually

Choose from professional presets or fine-tune your image with our advanced color grading controls. See changes in real-time.

3. Optimize & Download

Adjust compression settings to balance quality and file size, then download your perfectly color-graded image with one click.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Start with a high-quality image for best results
  • Use subtle adjustments for natural-looking results
  • Try different presets to discover new styles
  • For web use, quality between 70-85 is usually optimal
  • WebP format offers best compression for modern browsers
  • Use the comparison slider to see before/after differences
